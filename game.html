Dreambound

Dreambound is a captivating analogy-based puzzle adventure that challenges your wit and problem-solving skills. Trapped in a surreal dream world, you must navigate through layers of intricate illusions, deciphering hidden meanings and symbolic puzzles to find your way back to reality.

With a total of 49 days of gameplay, Dreambound unfolds over five progressively challenging dreamscapes, each pushing the boundaries of logic and perception:

Dream 1: Day 1 to Day 10 – The Fog of Uncertainty
Dream 2: Day 11 to Day 20 – Fragments of Memory
Dream 3: Day 21 to Day 30 – The Paradox Maze
Dream 4: Day 31 to Day 40 – Echoes of the Subconscious
Dream 5: Day 41 to Day 49 – The Final Awakening

Each night, you must solve an enigmatic puzzle before the dream consumes you. As you progress, the challenges become more complex, demanding deeper insight and abstract thinking.

Will you decipher the meaning behind your dreams, unlock every hidden pathway, and finally awaken? The journey through the mind begins now.
